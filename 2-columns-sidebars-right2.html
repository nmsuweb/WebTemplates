Economic Impact Report (Heading 2)

For more than 100 years, NMSU has been a major economic engine for the state. Even during challenging fiscal times, the university pours thousands of jobs and millions of dollars into New Mexico's economy. "The Economic Impact of New Mexico State University in 2010" provides ample evidence of NMSU's invaluable contributions. One finding of note: NMSU alumni who remain in the state account for hundres of millions of dollars in income and tax revenue.
